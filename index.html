ABOUT


Suki Lau is a Hong Kong born, Salt Lake City based illustrator. Her work often contains a narrative enhanced with a clever use of light that allows the images to tell a story and makes the spectator think. Suki is currently working on some more personal pieces including paintings and objects. I love drawing, making GIFs, watching series, cooking soup and drinking coffee. I’m a healthy food lover, and cat person. My illustration is all about human shapes, daily life situations, feelings, few colors and fun. I love expressing with the body and colors. I work combining analog and digital materials. I've done editorial illustration, character design and some experimental animation.
