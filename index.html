ABOUT


Suki Lau is a Hong Kong born, Salt Lake City based illustrator. Her work explores human shapes and daily life situation through both digital and tranditional mediums. Many of these illustrations represent moments of her life in Hong Kong where she was born and bred. Suki welcomes any voluntary project with NGOs and is currently working on some personal projects including paintings and motion graphics. If you have a sweet project or just want to say hello, feel free to get in touch through email or other networks!
