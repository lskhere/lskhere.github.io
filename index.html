Suki Lau is a visualization

I am a Hong Kong designer and illustrator based in Salt Lake City. I like telling stories with data and graphics. My work is mainly about doing data visualization based on open data. I also create illustrations, graphics and some animations. I started working with data and graphics 6 years ago as a statistician and amateur designer. I believe that by bringing information and visual aesthetics together, it would enhance communication and eventually enable people to better their lives.
