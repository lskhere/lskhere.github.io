Hi, I'm Suki


I am a Hong Kong illustrator/designer based in Salt Lake City. I create illustrations, graphics, websites and some animations. Before becoming an illustrator/designer, I was a statistician. I like telling stories with data and graphics. I believe that by bringing information and visual aesthetics together, it would enhance communication and eventually enable people to better their lives. That is why I am especially interested in creating visual content and informative graphic materials.
