Hong Kong Cha Chaan Teng

Illustration

This is an illustration series of authentic Hong Kong cha chaan teng experience. Cha chaan teng (literally means tea restaurant) finds its origins in post-WWII where Hong Kong culture was influenced by Bristish culture. It is a type of Hong Kong-style Western restaurants popular among locals because of its affordable menus.
