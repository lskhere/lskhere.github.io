Identity Branding for Music Start-Up

branding / logo design / illustration

This is a branding development project for a music start-up. The project includes a mind map visualizing the brainstorming process, contemporary mood board and historical mood board presenting image references to form the “look and feel” for the start-up, design process of the logotype, logo, color palette and modular system. The process also explores potential brand applications of the design. A branding devleopment guide presenting the aforesaid deisgn process is availaile here.
